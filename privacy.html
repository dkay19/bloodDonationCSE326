Indianblooddonors.com is a helpline which functions with the sole aim to promote and spread awareness about blood donation and connects needy patients with blood donors.

Contacting Blood Donors

The users need to utilise the Donors name and Telephone number provided via IVRS, SMS and Android and Windows APP and contact the donor for blood donation. The details of the donors are given only to facilitate the users for contacting them when in need of blood in case of any emergency. Indianblooddonors.com is not responsible for unwillingness of the donors to donate blood.
